What's TwitterDiaries


What's wrong

As we progress in life, we often only look forward and never backwords. With the advancement of technology, this increased the pace of life, forcing one to only move forward, forgetting the past achievements, failures, personal growth and events that shaped you and your values.

What TwitterDiaries does

The team here at TwitterDiaries finds this problem imperative and hence created a solution for it — TwitterDiaries, a one stop platform to learn more about yourself and your progress. It is user-friendly, visually appealing and upholds data privacy.

Features

TwitterDiaries has a range of features that will help you to better understand yourself.
We use Twitter API to extract tweets from your Twitter Account and then analyse them with a few of our self-invented functions.

Personalised Dashboard

A wonderful, personalized dashboard for you to view importants stats about yourself that you never knew.

Sentiment Analysis

Twitter Diaries can analyse the sentiments of your tweets — positive, neutral, negative and present them in visual charts.

Popular Tweets

Find out your most popular tweets! — by number of likes/comments

Visual Charts

You can now see your stats in beautiful charts with filters that cater to your specific needs.

Help Suggestions

TwitterDiaries can provide different types of suggestions according to the analysis.

Easy to Use

Just a few clicks or buttons and you will get the result that you need/want!
